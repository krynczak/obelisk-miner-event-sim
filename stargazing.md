Stargazing is unlocked after beating Obelisk 23. You can unlock individual Stars in the Stargazing menu to collect them and upgrade the buffs they give. Stars provide a variety of bonuses to all other areas of the game (except [[Archaeology]]), and are also used for [[Fishing#Legendary Fish|Legendary Fish Tributes]].

{{Clear|left}}
==Catching Stars==
To start collecting a Star, you must upgrade your Telescope in the upgrades tab of the menu, and then unlock the Star in the Stars tab. Stars have a base 1/50 chance to spawn every time the floor they have been sighted on is cleared. The floor changes at xx:00 and xx:30 on the clock, i.e., every 30 minutes. When a regular Star spawns there is a base 1/100 chance for a Super Star to spawn in addition to the normal Star (you do not need to unlock Super Stars). Your Star Spawn Rate Multiplier will increase the amount of Super Stars that spawn, but stats like Double Star Chance won't.

The floor change can be "canceled" by exiting the game before the floor change occurs and re-opening it after. Alternatively, the "[[Skill-Tree#Skills|Ctrl-F 'Stars']]" Skill allows you to automatically follow a Star when it moves to a new floor. You must enable this feature by double-tapping the Star sighting icon on the main screen or the map icon in the Stars tab of the Stargazing menu.

At first, you have to manually collect the Stars by tapping them as they slowly travel across the screen. Auto-catch can be gained permanently from [[Stargazing#Stars|Taurus]] and [[Stargazing#Upgrades|Stargazing upgrades]], or temporarily from [[Lootbugs]], the [[Drones#Elixir Drone Buffs|Elixir Drone]], or the fueled [[Drones#Drone Buffs|Starburst drone.]]

==Stargazing Stats==
See the corresponding section on the [[Stats#Stars|Stats Page]] for sources of the following stats:
*Star Spawn Rate Multiplier: Multiplicatively increases the spawn rate of regular stars
*Star Auto-Catch: Chance for a star or super star to be automatically caught
*Double Star Chance: Chance for 2 stars to spawn at once
*Triple Star Chance: Chance for 3 stars to spawn at once
*Super Star Spawn Rate Multiplier: Multiplicatively increases the spawn rate of super stars
*Super Star Triple Chance: Chance for 3 super stars to spawn
*Super Star 10x Spawn Chance: Chance for 10 super stars to spawn
*Star Supernova Chance: Stars can supernova, giving 10x stars
*Star Supernova Multiplier: Increases the multiplier of Star Supernova
*Super Star Supernova Chance: Super Stars can supernova, giving 10x super stars
*Super Star Supernova Multiplier: Increases the multiplier of Super Star Supernova
*Star Supergiant Chance: Stars can Supergiant, giving 3x stars
*Star Supergiant Multiplier: Increases the multiplier of Star Supergiant
*Super Star Supergiant Chance: Super Stars can Supergiant, giving 3x super stars
*Super Star Supergiant Multiplier: Increases the multiplier of Super Star Supergiant
*Star Upgrade Cap: Increases the maximum upgrade level of stars
*Star Radiant Chance: Chance Stars can be Radiant, giving 10x stars
*Star Radiant Multiplier: Increases the multiplier of Star Radiant
*Super Star Radiant Chance: Chance Super Stars can be Radiant, giving 10x super stars
*Super Star Radiant Multiplier: Increases the multiplier of Super Star Radiant
*All Star Multiplier: Increases income per star caught for Stars and Super Stars
*Novagiant Combo Multiplier: Multiplies income when a Star or Superstar is both Supernova and Supergiant

==Stars== 
There are 3 sections of Stars which can be divided by their unlock order:

# Aries is always your first unlocked star, Taurus is always your second one, and Gemini is always your third one;
# From Cancer to Aquarius (4th - 11th) the order is random;
# Pisces - always 12, Ophiuchus - 13, Orion - 14, Hercules - 15 ,Draco - 16, Cetus - 17, Phoenix - 18, and Eridanus - 19.

Each star has to be unlocked (discovered) first and then can be collected to upgrade its bonus. The discovery cost is 1 gem for the first Star (Aries) and then 250*(N+1) gems where N is the number of Stars you already have for subsequent Stars. The upgrade cost for each Star increases by 1.25x per level (for most of stars).

==Black Hole==
The Black Hole provides permanent bonuses in return for a large amount of a randomly selected Vein (Stone Vein - Neon Vein), a random early Star (Aries - Pisces) and a random late Star (Ophiuchus - Cetus). The amount required is also randomized by +/- 15% from the average. Playing on [[Update:V1.9.0]] rolled the cost for the first level with higher variance and saved the value, even if Black Hole wasn't unlocked until a later patch. You may see values higher or lower than those stated for the first Black Hole level if this is the case.
